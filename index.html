Wild Technologies

Wild Technologies operates U.S.-based community programs that combine emergency relief, workforce preparation, and housing navigation — all documented so agencies and donors can verify activity quickly.

What we do

We meet people where they are — on the street, at temporary sites, or through referrals. From there we connect them to services, casework, and employer partners. Each site, address, and contact line is public and tied to our EIN 10346569.

Programs are designed to be repeatable so local governments can scale them without losing transparency.

Program tracks

Relief & Outreach

Food, hygiene, navigation to services, and short-term stabilization run in collaboration with local providers.

Workforce & Employer Links

Paid transitional crews, certifications, and warm hand-offs to employers who signed clear MOUs.

Land / Housing Activation

Support for interim safe sites and pathways to permanent homes, with operating rules posted.
